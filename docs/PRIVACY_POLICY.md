# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 13, 2026

**WifiHomeView Developer** ("we", "us", or "our") operates the WifiHomeView mobile application (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

## Information Collection and Use

**We do NOT collect, transmit, distribute, or sell your personal data.** 

WifiHomeView is designed to operate entirely locally on your device. The primary function of the app is to scan your local network to discover connected devices.

### Permissions Requested

To function correctly, the application requires the following permissions:

*   **Location Permissions (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION):** Android requires location permissions to access the name (SSID) of the Wi-Fi network you are currently connected to. We do not track your physical location.
*   **Network State Permissions (ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE / ACCESS_WIFI_STATE):** Required to determine if you are connected to a Wi-Fi network and to retrieve local IP address information for scanning.
*   **Internet Permission (INTERNET):** Required to perform local network requests (e.g., Ping, ARP) to discover devices on your local subnet.

**None of the data discovered during these scans (such as device IP addresses, MAC addresses, or network names) is ever sent to our servers.** All data remains locally on your device.

## Third-Party Services

Our application does not integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. 

## Security of Data

We value your trust in using our application. Since no data is transmitted off your device, the security of your network data is entirely under your control.
